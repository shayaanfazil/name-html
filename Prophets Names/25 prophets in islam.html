There are 25 prophets in Islam, starting with Adam and ending with Muhammad 1. The following is a list of all the prophets in Islam:

  1. Adam
  2. Idris (Enoch)
  3. Nuh (Noah)
  4. Hud
  5. Salih
  6. Ibrahim (Abraham)
  7. Isma’il (Ishmael)
  8. Ishaq (Isaac)
  9. Yaqub (Jacob)
  10. Yusuf (Joseph)
  11. Shu’aib (Jethro)
  12. Ayyub (Job)
  13. Dhul-Kifl (Ezekiel)
  14. Musa (Moses)
  15. Harun (Aaron)
  16. Dawud (David)
  17. Sulayman (Solomon)
  18. Ilyas (Elias)
  19. Al-Yasa’ (Elisha)
  20. Yunus (Jonah)
  21. Zakariyya (Zechariah)
  22. Yahya (John the Baptist)
  23. Isa (Jesus)
  24. Uzair (Ezra)
  25. Muhammad
