Adam (Arabic: آدم, romanized: ʾĀdam) is the first human being on Earth and the first prophet of Islam . According to Islamic theology, Allah created Adam and his wife, Hawwa (Eve), and placed them in the Garden of Eden. Allah commanded them not to eat from the tree of knowledge, but they disobeyed and were banished from the garden . Adam is considered a revered figure in Islam and is mentioned in the Quran as one of the prophets .

There are several accounts of Adam’s life in Islamic literature. One such account states that Allah created Adam from clay and breathed life into him . Another account states that Allah created Adam in His own image and taught him the names of all things . According to Islamic tradition, Adam lived for about 1000 years after his creation
